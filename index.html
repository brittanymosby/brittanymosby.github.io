In addition to the 0530 Workbook, the following websites offer a good refresher for algebra topics including decimals and percents, solving equations and inequalities, order of operations, solving proportions and working with fractions.

MATH 1530 - Introductory Statistics

PSCC Instructors have created a resource page for 1530 students, which includes links to videos, an open source textbook, online stats calculators, and a MyOpenMath course for additional practice.
